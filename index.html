  • History of Planes

    • The history of planes dates back to the early 20th century, with the Wright brothers' first flight in 1903. Since then, planes have become faster, more efficient, and more reliable. Today, they are an essential part of modern transportation and play a vital role in many industries, including tourism, logistics, and defense.
